01 — Our Process

How we invest

A disciplined, fundamentals-first approach to finding and executing on acquisition and repositioning opportunities.

The Four Steps

Source
Underwrite
Acquire
Reposition

We apply rigorous standards at every stage, maintaining a high bar for deal quality and counterparty relationships.

01

Source

We identify potential acquisitions through a combination of direct owner outreach, partner networks, and market analysis. Our sourcing focuses on supply-constrained, high-demand residential markets with strong fundamentals and long-term demographic tailwinds.

02

Underwrite

Every opportunity goes through rigorous financial and operational analysis. We evaluate property condition, market dynamics, rental potential, and repositioning costs. We only advance to acquisition when fundamentals align with our return requirements.

03

Acquire

We close all-cash transactions with no financing contingencies. Our capital availability and streamlined decision-making mean we can move quickly and reliably. Most closings occur within 3 weeks of going under contract.

04

Reposition

Post-acquisition, we manage active repositioning—either through rental income optimization, strategic improvements, or both. Our goal is to enhance property fundamentals and unlock value for the long term.
