The process

Four steps, fundamentals first.

From first contact to a closed transaction, our process is built for certainty — for us and for the people we work with.

Most of what makes a transaction work is decided long before closing. We start from the numbers: the basis, the location, the path to value. When an opportunity clears that bar, we move with terms designed to remove friction — all-cash, no financing contingencies, and a timeline that suits the seller. The four steps below are how a fundamentals-first read becomes a clean, certain close.

01

Source

We identify opportunities directly and through a nationwide network of owners, brokers, and partners — off the open market wherever possible.

02

Underwrite

Every opportunity is evaluated on its fundamentals: entry basis, location, and a clear, defensible path to value before any offer is made.

03

Acquire

Clean, all-cash terms with flexible timelines and no financing contingencies. A straightforward agreement that closes when it says it will.

04

Reposition

We hold, improve, or reposition each asset toward its highest and best use — the step that turns a sound entry into a realized return.

We underwrite the numbers, not the noise — and we close what we put under contract.
