About the firm

A private, capital-backed platform built on discipline and execution.

We acquire, hold, and reposition residential property across the country — working directly with owners, partners, and intermediaries. We target supply-constrained markets with durable fundamentals, and we close every deal all-cash, on time, without drama.

What we look for

Our three filters
01
Property type

Residential

Single-family homes and small multifamily properties where we can add meaningful value through disciplined repositioning.

  • Single-family rental homes
  • Small multifamily (2–8 units)
  • Value-add & distressed
  • Off-market opportunities
02
Markets

Fundamentals-first

Markets with durable demand drivers — strong demographics, employment diversity, and limited supply relative to household formation.

  • Supply-constrained metros
  • High in-migration corridors
  • Long-term economic strength
  • All five U.S. regions
03
Deal structure

Clean & certain

All-cash closings with no financing contingencies. Our capital is committed, our decisions are fast, and our terms are clear.

  • 100% cash-funded
  • No financing contingencies
  • ~3 week close timeline
  • 72h inquiry response

The process

Four repeatable steps
01

Source

Direct owner outreach, partner networks, and market analysis — focused on supply-constrained, high-demand corridors.

02

Underwrite

Rigorous financial and operational analysis. Every deal clears a consistent set of return and risk criteria before we proceed.

03

Acquire

All-cash closings with no contingencies. Streamlined decision-making — most transactions close within 3 weeks.

04

Reposition

Post-acquisition value creation through rental optimization, strategic improvements, and long-term asset management.
